\section*{The golden ratio}
The symbol for the golden ratio is the Greek
letter \( \varphi \). Its value is the positive solution
of \( x^2 - x - 1 = 0 \).
It can be calculated to:
\[
  \varphi = \frac{1 + \sqrt{5}}{2} = 1.618 \ldots
\]
